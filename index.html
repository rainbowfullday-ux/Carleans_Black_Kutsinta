History

Carleans started in 2015 with husband and wife Rolan and Carla Paez, who shared their homemade kakanin online. Their black kutsinta became a hit for its unique color and tasty caramel and coconut toppings. From small beginnings, Carleans now has branches in Calumpang, Pantok, and Angono, with other Filipino favorites also available.
